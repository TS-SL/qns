About Queens Networking Society

Empowering students through meaningful professional connections

Our Mission

The Queens Networking Society is dedicated to fostering professional growth and career development among students at Queen's University. We believe that meaningful connections are the foundation of professional success, and we're committed to providing opportunities for students to build these relationships.

What We Do

Our society organizes regular networking events, workshops, and mentorship programs that connect students with industry professionals, alumni, and peers. We focus on creating an inclusive environment where everyone can develop their professional skills and expand their network.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: We welcome students from all backgrounds and disciplines
  • Professionalism: We maintain high standards in all our interactions
  • Growth: We prioritize continuous learning and development
  • Community: We build lasting relationships within our network
