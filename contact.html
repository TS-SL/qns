Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the Queens Networking Society?

You can join by attending one of our events or contacting us through this form. We welcome students from all faculties and years.

What types of events do you organize?

We organize networking mixers, industry panels, workshops, mentorship programs, and career development sessions throughout the academic year.

Is there a membership fee?

Basic membership is free for Queen's University students. Some premium events may have a small fee to cover costs.

How often do you meet?

We typically organize 2-3 events per month during the academic year, plus regular committee meetings for active members.
